# Instuctional Design Plan

---

## Purpose and Context

This instructional design plan demonstrates my ability to apply the ADDIE model and adult learning principles to design, develop, and deliver a live tutorial on screencast creation with OBS Studio. The project includes learner analysis, learning objectives, and a structured lesson plan and storyboard developed in Microsoft PowerPoint to ensure clear, practical, and measurable learning outcomes.

## Skills
- Conduct learner and task analysis to align instruction with performance outcomes
- Design a structured lesson plans and storyboard
- Integrate adult learning principles into training design
- Create step-by-step demonstrations and guided practice activities
- Align assessments and activities with learning objectives
